Controls for level editor: 

D - Enter Deletion / Creation mode
C - Exit Deletion / Creation mode
Click to delete. Click and hold a number to add an object.
Hold 0 - Drop a player
Hold 1 - Drop a block
Hold 2 - Drop a flag
Hold 3 - Drop a collectable
Hold 4 - Drop an enemy
S - Save to new
